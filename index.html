PROJECT REIK

Project Reik is an original superhero movie created by Ryan and Leeo Rodriguez from the Power Cousins X Studios. The story follows Reik, a young hero who was experimented on as a child and accidentally absorbed all the powers in the world. As his abilities grow, he must face a mysterious villain known as The Unknown, who uses shadow powers and has dark plans for Earth. Alongside a powerful team called The Legions, Reik fights to protect the world while uncovering secrets about his past, his long-lost brother Echo, and the future of humanity. Packed with action, emotion, and epic twists, Project Reik is the beginning of a powerful new superhero universe.
